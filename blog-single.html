Eco & Social priority

Rainforest & SEDEX - SMETA Certified

Success is sustainable growth

As employers we are aware of our important social role, a fundamental part of the flower industry, which is our motivation and commitment to our employees is to provide and ensure fair treatment and good working conditions, training programs continue under strict social responsibility and environmental care.

Sustainability policies


We have social, environmental and occupational health policies and programs giving each of these issues all the necessary importance. Our social responsibility policy provides support to our employees’ medical and dental services, counseling in family welfare and health all these services extended to their family groups for the sole purpose of improving their living conditions, our company also offers employees transportation and room service ensures a safe working conditions. We have an environmental policy that maintains the integrity of the ecosystems by preventing alteration or destruction through the implementation of support programs and continuous commitment work.
